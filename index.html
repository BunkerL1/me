A Small Manifesto on Internet Freedom

The internet is a global platform for free expression, collaboration, and innovation. It has become an integral part of our lives, empowering individuals to connect, share knowledge, and express themselves freely. Internet freedom is a fundamental right that should be protected and preserved for future generations.

Access to information, privacy, and the ability to communicate freely are essential elements of a free and open internet. We must strive to ensure that the internet remains a decentralized, neutral space where ideas can flourish without censorship or restriction.

Let us work together to defend internet freedom and uphold the principles of openness, transparency, and democracy online. Together, we can build a better future where the internet remains a beacon of hope and opportunity for all.
